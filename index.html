Welcome to Lake Mendota

Lake Mendota is one of the most beautiful lakes on the University of Wisconsin-Madison campus. During golden hour, the sky transforms into a stunning display of pink, blue, and orange hues that reflect perfectly on the calm waters.

This website showcases photography captured from the Memorial Union Terrace, where visitors gather to enjoy the scenic views and vibrant sunsets. Each photograph tells a story of the lake's natural beauty and the peaceful atmosphere that surrounds it.

Explore the gallery to see stunning imagery, learn about the science behind the beautiful colors in our about section, discover the best spots to visit, and find more information about UW-Madison.

For more information about the Memorial Union and its history, visit the official Memorial Union website.
