Sunset Photography Collection

These photographs were taken from the Memorial Union Terrace overlooking Lake Mendota. Each image captures a different moment during the golden hour, when the light transforms the landscape into something magical.

Each photograph tells a story of peaceful moments and natural beauty. The combination of soft lighting and reflective water creates a serene atmosphere that draws visitors to this special place on campus.
