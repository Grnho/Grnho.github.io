Best Photography Spots

The Memorial Union Terrace and surrounding areas offer excellent opportunities for sunset photography. Here are the best spots to capture stunning images of Lake Mendota.

Top Locations

  1. Memorial Union Terrace - The main terrace provides elevated views of the entire lake with the sunset directly ahead
  2. The Pier - Walk out onto the pier for unique angles with the water surrounding you on all sides
  3. Terrace Steps - Position yourself on the steps for varied perspectives that include both the terrace and the lake
  4. Lakeside Path - Stroll along the path for close-up water shots and different framing opportunities

Photography Tips

To get the most out of your visit, follow these helpful suggestions for capturing beautiful sunset photos.

Essential Preparation

  • Arrive early - Come 30-45 minutes before sunset to secure a good spot and watch the changing colors
  • Check the weather - Clear skies are best, but some clouds can add drama to your photos
  • Bring stability - Use a tripod or find a stable surface for sharper low-light shots
  • Watch your exposure - Sunsets can fool camera meters, so bracket your exposures or use manual mode
  • Include foreground interest - Add silhouettes of people, boats, or structures to create depth
  • Stay after sunset - The colors continue changing for 10-15 minutes after the sun goes down

For the latest information about visiting Memorial Union, check the Memorial Union official website for hours and special events.

What to Expect

Memorial Union is a popular gathering place, especially during nice weather. You'll likely see students studying, families enjoying the view, and photographers capturing the moment. The atmosphere is relaxed and welcoming, making it a perfect place to unwind and appreciate nature's beauty.
